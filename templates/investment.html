Risk Reduction Suggestions

Actionable Tips

  • Diversify your portfolio to reduce market exposure.
  • Invest in low-volatility assets for long-term stability.
  • Rebalance your portfolio periodically to maintain the right risk level.
  • Consider investing in bonds, mutual funds, or ETFs for less risk.

Suggested Diversification Strategies

  • For a conservative portfolio: 70% bonds, 30% stable stocks.
  • For a balanced portfolio: 50% stocks, 30% bonds, 20% alternative assets.
  • For an aggressive portfolio: 80% stocks, 20% bonds.
